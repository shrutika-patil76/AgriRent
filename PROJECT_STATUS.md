## 🎯 Project Overview

**Smart Agricultural Equipment Rental System** - A full-stack MERN application that connects farmers with equipment owners, enabling easy rental of agricultural machinery.

## ✅ Completed Features

### Authentication & Authorization
- ✅ Multi-role system (Farmer, Owner, Admin)
- ✅ JWT-based authentication
- ✅ Secure password hashing with bcrypt
- ✅ Protected routes and middleware

### Equipment Management
- ✅ Browse equipment with filters (category, location, price)
- ✅ Detailed equipment view with specifications
- ✅ Image upload functionality
- ✅ Owner can add/edit/delete equipment
- ✅ Availability status management

### Booking System
- ✅ Date-based booking with conflict prevention
- ✅ Real-time availability checking
- ✅ Display of already booked dates
- ✅ Booking workflow: Pending → Confirmed → Ongoing → Completed
- ✅ Owner approval/rejection system
- ✅ Payment calculation (rent + deposit)

### Reviews & Ratings

- ✅ Review count display
